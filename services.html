Our Sevices

At Dr Wash Laundry, we offer a comprehensive range of laundry services to meet all your needs. Our services include:

  • Washing and Drying: We provide high-quality washing and drying services using eco-friendly detergents and modern equipment to ensure your clothes are clean and fresh.
  • Ironing: Our professional ironing service will leave your clothes wrinkle-free and looking their best.
  • Dry Cleaning: We offer expert dry cleaning for delicate fabrics and special garments, ensuring they are treated with the utmost care.
  • Pickup and Delivery: For your convenience, we offer pickup and delivery services, making it easier than ever to get your laundry done.
  • Special Care Services: We provide specialized care for items such as suits, dresses, and other delicate clothing that require extra attention.
